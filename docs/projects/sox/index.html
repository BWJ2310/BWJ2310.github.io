Sox

A robot cat contains voice assistant and "cat -like" motion to reproduce the animated character "Sox" from Lightyear movie

The longest and biggest personal project I worked on from June 2022 through 2024.

Utilized Coqui TTS for generating customized voice synthesis, MyCroft for customized voice assistance, and BotPress to filter commands or normal chats for switching to GPT API to produce personality. Also includes facial recognition and distance measuring for better motion performance. The project ran from June 2022 through 2024.
